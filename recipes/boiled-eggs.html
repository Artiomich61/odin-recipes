Boiled Eggs

Description

A lot of dishes contain boiled eggs in recipes, so you need to know how to cook this chicken's product

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pan of water

Cooking steps

  1. Boil water in the pan
  2. Add salt into the water
  3. Put eggs in the boiling water for 3 minutes (if you want them to be soft-boiled) or 5 minutes (for hard-boiled eggs)
  4. After time is out, drain the water from the pot and wait for the eggs to cool down

Congratulate! Now you have some boiled eggs!
